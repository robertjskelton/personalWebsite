Global Datafest Hackathon Organizer 2015

I learned first hand how difficult it is to oragnize a weekend event for fifty participants. Organizing and running this hackathon with Song Gao and Ana Maria Carrano was so much fun. My opening speech PowerPoint is located here.

Global Datafest Hacakthon Participant 2013 and 2015

Groups I participated in won the Best Serving Alabama Award in 2013 and 3rd Place in 2015 for our projects Simulated Hurricane Migration Visualization and Smart Traffic Grid. Source code for both is on my Github.

Drove a Ferrari F430

While on study abroad in Florence, Italy, I drove a Ferrari F430 in Maranello at the site of the Ferrari Museum. It's ridiculously fast. I also saw my dream car in person, the legendary Ferrari Enzo. During this trip, I visited 14 countries throughout Western Europe, and vastly improved my understanding of the world.

China Trip

I also studied abroad in Beijing and Taiyuan, China in the Summer of 2014. I walked the Great Wall of China, the single most impressive thing I've ever seen, tried Peking Duck, and learned about the Chinese Culture. I didn't eat Chinese food for two months after I returned, but it was a great experience.

Met Titan

I worked an internship at Oak Ridge National Laboratory after I returned from China. I got to execute a few commands on Titan, the second fastest supercomputer in the world and the fastest in America. My project was to develop a process to create a cyber range, so that the scientists at ORNL had a safe environment to test malware. The project paper is located here.

Personal

In my down time, I like to enjoy craft beers, play and collect vintage video games, create music, and consume media.

My favorite styles of beer are Imperial Stouts, followed by Double IPAs and sours. My favorite beers I've tried are Westbrook's Mexican Cake, Russian River's Supplication, and Good People's Snake Handler. One of my life goals is to try the Top 250 beers in the world, as rated by BeerAdvocate. Friend me on Untappd!

I've been collecting vintage video games for a few years now. My favorite systems are the SNES and PS2. My favorite games are usually JRPGs, and Chrono Trigger is my favorite so far. I love playing games on their original systems, but since internal batteries of Gameboy games are dying and Playstation memory cards become corrupt so often, I do most of my gaming on my Raspberry Pi 2 now.

As far as music goes, I've been playing guitar since 2004, drums since 2007, and harmonica since 2011. Led Zeppelin is my favorite band, and I'd empty out my bank account to see them perform live. My main axe is a Fender Showmaster. I've had around 50 guitars, and it's the only one I'll never sell.

Another life goal is watch the Top 250 movies, as rated by IMDB. My favorite movies are School of Rock, Ferris Bueller's Day Off, and Spaceballs, so obviously I really like comedies. I've also seen every episode of South Park. Twice.
